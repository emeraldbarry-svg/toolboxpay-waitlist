Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At ToolboxPay, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you join our waitlist.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect the information you voluntarily provide through our Tally form, which typically includes your name, email address, trade, and admin habits.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this information to:

  • Send you early access invites and updates about ToolboxPay.
  • Understand our audience to build better features for the trades.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored via Tally.so and will never be sold to third parties. We only keep your data for as long as necessary to provide you with access to the platform.

4. Your Rights

As a UK resident, you have the right to request access to the data we hold about you or ask us to delete it at any time. Simply contact us via our support channels.
